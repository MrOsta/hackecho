Why Hackecho

Hackecho will provide an ideal platform to developers, graphic designers and other technology professionals to collaborate on different IT projects.

  • It will shorten the innovation cycle.
  • It will attract people with diverse skills to work together.
  • It will help in driving innovation.
  • It will incur lesser costs compared to traditional innovation models.
  • It will move ideas to prototypes in just a few days

Hackecho will help in arriving at new solutions in just a matter of hours, which can then be quickly executed.It is one of the best ways to drive innovation internally by engaging with your fellow mates. It provides a platform for you and your mates to collaborate with other business functions and showcase their talent.
Hackecho is the tool that covers 70% of the innovation journey. It starts with discovery, idea generation, and ends with idea conceptualization.
It allows you to rapidly prototype an idea, determine the quality of the idea, spot flaws, reiterate, scale it, and roll it out to the masses.
